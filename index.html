Classic Rock & R&B · Hobart, Tasmania

Five-piece party band delivering funky classic rock and R&B favourites from the sixties to the noughties. Lively dance rhythms, lush vocal harmonies, and sonic textures — from smooth ballads to full-throttle floor-fillers.

The Band

Meet SoundBites

Frontman Sean delivers lead vocals and acoustic guitar, backed by Paul on bass, Sally on keys, Simon on lead guitar, and Trevor on drums. With four singers in the lineup, the band brings rich vocal harmonies across every set.
