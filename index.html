Classic Rock & R&B · Hobart, Tasmania

Five-piece party band delivering funky classic rock and R&B favourites from the sixties to the noughties. Lively dance rhythms, lush vocal harmonies, and sonic textures — from smooth ballads to full-throttle floor-fillers.

The Band

Meet SoundBites

Frontman Sean engages with lead vocals and acoustic guitar, supported by Paul, Sally, Simon, and Trevor. Four singers conjuring a rich variety of songs and lush harmonies.

We pride ourselves on sounding great at a volume that allows conversation for patrons who are dining — but with the energy to draw them onto the dance floor. Of course, we can turn up the volume later when suitable.
